200 Hour Teacher Training

January 3rd, 2020 - April 26th, 2020

The MOXIE 200 Hour Yoga Alliance Teacher Training will prepare students to teach private and group power yoga classes.  Students will focus on teaching methodology, anatomy and physiology, yoga philosophy, lifestyle and ethics, as well as practicum including teaching with observation and instruction by MOXIE's instructors.

Trainers: Mark Rausa, Lauren Marmol, Katrina Baccetti, Colleen April and Laura Della Guardia
Location: MOXIE: Bernal Heights Location at 3232 Mission Street, San Francisco, CA.
Date & Time:  January 3rd to April 26th, Fridays from 6:45pm - 9
:15pm; Saturdays and Sundays from 12:30pm-4:30pm (No Meeting Sessions on January 17th-19th, February 14th-16th, April 10th-12th)
Tuition:  Early Bird: $2000 (Before 11.30.19) Standard Rate: $2500
Four Months of Unlimited Classes are Included (January through April 2020)

Advanced Practice

The Advanced Practice Training is a multiple weekend intensive designed to help break down advanced postures and transitions to help take your yoga practice to the next level through a carefully developed series of workshops and advanced practice classes.  Each day will consist of a workshop and advanced practice where you will apply the skills learned.

ADVANCED PRACTICE INCLUDES

• Backbending• Hip Opening• Arm Balancing• Transitions• Binding• Handstands• Inversions
