Zoom Live Streaming Instructions

  1. Sign up for your class through our Mindbody Online Class Schedule or our App (https://apps.apple.com/us/app) - Make sure to select "20th Street" as your location.
  2. Open the email you received from your instructor and click the Zoom link (this email will come 15 minutes before class begins)
  3. Download Zoom on your computer or Smart Phone if not already installed.
  4. Enter the meeting ID and Password provided in the email
  5. Join the class (choose "join without video")

The MOXIE Method

Our signature approach to fitness-inspired yoga.

MOXIE FUNDAMENTALS

55-minute class.  Essential postures of yoga that affect the entire body.  Deep focus on alignment and breath connection.  Postures are held 8-10 breaths.  Sequence follows the MOXIE Fundamental postures.  Appropriate for all levels.  This is not a beginner only class: all students interested in working on the principles and foundations of Yoga should attend.

SEE SCHEDULE

MOXIE METHOD

55-minute class based on the Fundamental postures.  A Truly All Levels class for beginner, intermediate, and advanced practitioners.  Modifications are provided for the beginners.  Variations offered for the more experienced.  Postures are held for 5-7 breaths.

SEE SCHEDULE

POWER YOGA

55-minute class.  Classes vary based on the instructor.  Accessible to All Levels.  Modifications provided for beginners.  Variations for the more experienced.
